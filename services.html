OUR SERVICES

Training is a crucial aspect of personal and professional development, enhancing skills, knowledge, and performance.
There are several types of training, each catering to different needs and objectives.
Here are some of the primary types of training, along with their purposes and methods:.

PURPOSE

To provide employees with practical, hands-on experience in their actual work environment.
To develop skills and knowledge in a setting separate from the workplace.
To teach specific technical skills or knowledge required for a job.
To improve interpersonal and communication skills.
To ensure employees understand and adhere to laws, regulations, and company policies.
To develop leadership and management skills.

METHODS

  • Job Shadowing: Trainees observe experienced employees performing their tasks.
  • Mentoring: Experienced employees guide and support less experienced ones.
  • Job Rotation: Employees rotate through different positions to learn various aspects of the business..
  • Classroom Training: Structured courses with lectures and discussions.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Interactive sessions focusing on specific skills.
  • Online Courses: Web-based training modules.
  • Certifications: Professional courses leading to certification (e.g., IT certifications).
  • Hands-On Practice: Practical sessions using tools or software.
  • Technical Workshops: Focused sessions on technical topics.
  • Workshops: Interactive sessions on topics like communication, teamwork, and leadership.
  • Role-Playing: Simulated scenarios to practice soft skills.
  • Coaching: One-on-one sessions focusing on personal development.
